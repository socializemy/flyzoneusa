What Is Fly Zone?

Fly Zone USA is a cultural platform built to bring real conversations to the forefront. Hosted by Detroit's own Trick Trick, the show connects culture, politics, and community in a way that speaks directly to everyday Americans.

We create a space where leaders, artists, and voices from across the country can talk openly about the issues shaping our future — no filter, no scripts, no games.
