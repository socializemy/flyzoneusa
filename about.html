About Fly Zone

What Is Fly Zone USA?

Fly Zone USA is a media platform built to bring real conversations about culture, politics, and opportunity to a broader audience.

Hosted by Detroit artist Trick Trick, the show connects voices from music, sports, business, and politics to discuss the issues shaping the future of America.

The goal is simple: create a space where honest conversations can happen. No teleprompters. No corporate scripts. Just raw, authentic dialogue from inside the culture.

The Fly Zone Ecosystem

Three interconnected entities. One unified mission to reach communities that traditional messaging cannot.

Fly Zone USA

LLC • Media Platform

The content engine. Fly Zone USA produces and distributes podcast episodes, short-form digital video, cultural interviews, and event content. YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and X are the primary platforms — delivering raw, unfiltered conversations that feel real, not rehearsed.

Fly Zone Action

501(c)(4) • Civic Engagement

Translating cultural engagement into civic awareness and voter engagement. Fly Zone Action turns conversation into consequence — connecting audiences to the political process through a lens they actually trust.

Fly Zone Institute

501(c)(3) • Education & Impact

The Institute focuses on education, research, and community programs that support opportunity and civic literacy. Education access, economic opportunity, and community leadership development — solutions-oriented, always.

Trick Trick

Host • Detroit Artist • Entrepreneur

Detroit artist, entrepreneur, and cultural leader. Known for protecting Detroit's culture and speaking truth without apology.

Through Fly Zone USA, Trick Trick is bringing real conversations directly to the people. His cultural credibility and audience trust allow Fly Zone to reach communities that traditional political messaging cannot touch.

The Team

Anton Castaneda — Founder, Managing Partner & Executive Producer. The strategic architect of the Fly Zone ecosystem. Responsible for partnerships, political strategy, and organizational structure.

Vincent Peters — Senior Advisor and partner supporting ecosystem strategy.
